Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

Solo OS ("we", "our", "the app") is built by Timur Mone. This policy explains how we handle your data.

TL;DR: Your data stays on your device by default. We don't sell your data. Period.

1. Data We Collect

Solo OS collects and stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Profile information - Your name (entered during onboarding)
  • Projects & tasks - Project names, task descriptions, completion status
  • Financial data - Income, expenses, obligations you enter manually
  • Habits - Habit names and daily completion records
  • Ideas - Idea titles, descriptions, and AI validation results
  • Contacts - Names, birthdays, and relationships you add
  • Daily standup logs - Your wins, challenges, and priorities text
  • Voice recordings - Processed on-device for speech-to-text; audio is not stored or transmitted

2. How Data Is Stored

By default, all data is stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences. Data never leaves your phone unless you explicitly opt in to cloud sync.

If you choose to sign in with Supabase (optional), your data is synced to a secured Supabase database to enable cross-device access. Supabase uses row-level security and encrypted connections.

3. Third-Party Services

Solo OS integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Anthropic Claude API - When you use AI features (daily digest, standup analysis, idea validation), your text input is sent to Anthropic's API for processing. Anthropic's privacy policy applies. We use a rate-limited proxy or your own API key.
  • Google Calendar & Contacts - If you connect your Google account, we access your calendar events and contact birthdays via Google APIs. We only read and create calendar events; we do not modify or delete your existing data.
  • RevenueCat - For subscription management. RevenueCat processes purchase receipts from Apple/Google. No personal data beyond purchase status is shared.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play - Standard platform billing for subscriptions.

4. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect analytics or usage tracking data
  • We do not use advertising SDKs
  • We do not collect device identifiers for profiling
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties
  • We do not store voice recordings - speech is processed on-device

5. Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time from Settings > Danger Zone > Reset All Data. This permanently removes all locally stored data. If you use Supabase sync, you can also delete your account by contacting us.

6. Children's Privacy

Solo OS is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
