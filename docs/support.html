FAQ

Q: Do I need an internet connection?
Solo OS works fully offline. AI features require internet, but all other features (projects, habits, finance, calendar) work without it.

Q: How do I reset my data?
Go to Settings > Danger Zone > Reset All Data. This deletes everything on-device.

Q: Can I use my own Claude API key?
Yes! Go to Settings > AI Settings and paste your key from console.anthropic.com. This gives you unlimited AI calls.

Q: How do I restore my subscription?
Go to Settings > Subscription > Upgrade to Pro, then tap "Restore Purchases" at the bottom.

Q: Is my financial data secure?
All data is stored locally on your device by default. We never upload your financial data to our servers.
